Most Esteemed Friend,

I beg your pardon for the tardiness of this humble note,  
for the day of your birth hath passed, and I, alas, am but late in my felicitations.

Nonetheless, my wishes are no less sincere, nor my regard diminished.

May the year ahead unfold with grace, joy, and quiet triumphs,  
and may each day be touched with gladness and good health.

Your presence in this world is a most welcome one,  
and it is my honor to send you these belated, yet heartfelt, congratulations.

With all due respect and friendly affection,
